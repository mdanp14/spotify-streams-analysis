# spotify-streams-analysis
Data analysis project exploring Spotify’s most streamed songs to uncover relationships between audio features (energy, loudness, danceability) and song popularity using Python, Pandas, and visualization libraries.

## Key Insights
- Most streamed songs tend to have moderate-to-high energy levels (0.5–0.8)
- The majority of popular songs fall within a 3–4 minute duration
- Loudness and energy show a positive relationship, suggesting louder songs are often more energetic
- Danceability is generally high among top songs, indicating strong rhythmic appeal
